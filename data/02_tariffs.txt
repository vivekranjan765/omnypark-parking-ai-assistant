Parking Tariffs - Demo Policy

Question: What are the parking charges per hour?
Answer: The standard demo parking tariff is AED 20 per additional hour after the free parking period ends.

Question: Are the parking charges the same at every location?
Answer: Not always. For this demo assistant, a standard tariff policy is used. In real operations, charges may differ by location, property type, operator policy, and promotional arrangements.

Demo Tariff Structure:
- Free parking duration: 3 hours
- Charge after free period: AED 20 per hour
- Additional hourly charge: AED 20 per hour
- Daily maximum charge: Not defined in this demo
- Lost ticket or special case fee: Not defined in this demo

Example:
If a location offers 3 free hours and charges AED 20 per additional hour, a customer staying for 5 hours would normally pay for 2 additional hours.

Customer Guidance:
- Always check the tariff board at the parking facility entrance, pay station, or official parking app for the final applicable charge.
- Tariffs may vary by site in real deployments.
- This assistant uses a simple demonstration tariff unless a specific site policy is added later.
