OmnyPark Public Parking Assistant - Overview

This assistant provides general public guidance related to parking at OmnyPark-managed or OmnyPark-supported parking locations in Dubai and the UAE.

OmnyPark provides parking management solutions for locations such as malls, commercial complexes, corporate parks, pay-and-park facilities, and residential complexes.

CASS Parking provides parking-related systems and technologies including parking management systems, payment options, parking guidance systems, smartphone applications, RFID-based access control, and ANPR-based access control.

This chatbot is designed for public users who want quick answers to common parking questions such as:
- parking charges per hour
- free parking duration
- cinema or outlet validation rules
- weekend and public holiday parking rules
- payment guidance
- general customer parking FAQs

This assistant is intended to provide general informational guidance only.
Actual parking rules may vary by location, mall, outlet, property owner, event, campaign, public holiday notification, or parking operator policy.

If there is any difference between the chatbot answer and the actual site signage or official operator policy, the site signage and official policy should be treated as final.
