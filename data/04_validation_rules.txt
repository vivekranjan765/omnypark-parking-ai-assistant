Validation Rules - Demo Policy

Question: What is parking validation?
Answer: Parking validation is a process where a participating outlet, cinema, merchant, or service desk extends or adjusts the parking entitlement of a customer according to the site's parking policy.

Question: Where can parking be validated?
Answer: In this demo, parking may be validated at designated participating outlets, cinema counters, or customer service desks, depending on the site's operating policy.

Question: Can cinema visitors get extra parking time?
Answer: Yes. In this demo, cinema visitors may receive 1 additional validated hour beyond the standard free parking duration.

Demo Validation Assumption:
- Standard free parking duration: 3 hours
- Additional cinema validation duration: 1 hour
- Total entitlement after cinema validation: 4 hours

Question: Is validation automatic?
Answer: Validation may be automatic or manual depending on the parking system design and location policy. In many cases, the user must complete validation at an approved point before exit.

Question: Can every store validate parking?
Answer: No. Only participating or authorized outlets can provide validation, and only according to the site's configured rules.

Question: Can validation be used after payment?
Answer: This depends on the site policy. In this demo, users are advised to validate before making payment or before exiting.

Question: Does validation policy differ by outlet?
Answer: Yes. In real deployments, validation benefits may vary by outlet, cinema, merchant category, spending threshold, or tenant-specific agreement.

Assistant Guidance:
- For this demo, use the cinema rule of 1 extra hour.
- Do not guarantee that every outlet provides validation.
