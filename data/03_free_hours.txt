Free Parking Duration - Demo Policy

Question: How many free parking hours are available?
Answer: The standard free parking duration in this demo is 3 hours.

Question: Does everyone get free parking?
Answer: In this demo, the assistant assumes public visitors receive 3 hours of free parking unless a specific site rule overrides it.

Question: When does paid parking start?
Answer: Paid parking starts after the 3-hour free parking period ends, unless the user receives a valid extension or validation from an authorized outlet or service point.

Question: Can the free parking duration change on weekends, holidays, or special events?
Answer: Yes. In real operations, free parking duration may change based on weekend policy, public holiday policy, promotional campaigns, or site-specific rules.

Guidance for the Assistant:
- Use 3 hours as the default free parking duration for this demo.
- If site-specific data is added later, the assistant should use the site-specific rule instead of the demo rule.
